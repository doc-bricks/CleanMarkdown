### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Where are my settings stored?**
Settings are stored locally via QSettings. On Windows, the configuration is in the registry under `HKCU\Software\CleanMarkdown`.

**Which file formats are supported?**
`.md`, `.markdown` and `.txt` files can be opened, edited and saved.

**How does PDF export work?**
Via `File â†’ Export as PDF`, the current preview is saved locally as a PDF. No external service is used.

**Can I switch between light and dark theme?**
Yes, via `View â†’ Settings` you can switch between light and dark theme.

**Are mathematical formulas supported?**
Yes. Inline formulas (`$...$`, `\(...\)`) and block formulas (`$$...$$`, `\[...\]`) are rendered in the preview.

**Does the app require an internet connection?**
No. CleanMarkdown works entirely offline. No data is transmitted.

**Is there an autosave feature?**
Yes. The autosave function can be enabled or disabled in the settings.

**How does scroll synchronization work?**
The optional scroll sync aligns the scroll position between reading and editor view. Enable it under `View â†’ Settings`.

- Windows 10 or newer
- Python 3.10+ (when running from source)
